# Product Requirements Document (PoC Version)

## 1. Member Onboarding
- **LinkedIn Integration:** Users provide a LinkedIn URL or PDF export.
- **The "Statement":** A 2-sentence requirement: "One thing I am currently struggling with" and "One thing I love helping others with."
- **Data Parsing:** AI must extract "Expertise," "Gaps," and "Human Sparks" (hobbies/interests).

## 2. The Matching Engine
- **Logic:** Must prioritize "Expertise-to-Gap" matches. 
- **Cadence:** Automated scheduling for twice-weekly 45-minute sessions.
- **Notification:** Email/Push notification 1 hour before with a "Connection Briefing."

## 3. In-Meeting Facilitation
- **The Sidebar UI:** Real-time display of member bios and AI-generated icebreakers.
- **Phase Timer:** A visual countdown for the 4 phases (Synthesis, Un-Pitch, Brain Trust, Action).
- **Post-Meeting Follow-up:** Automated email with "Next Steps" and an AI-drafted "Scary Reach Out" message.

## 4. Community Manager Dashboard
- **Analytics:** Connection counts, "Luck Score" (sentiment analysis of meeting value).
- **Branding:** Upload logo, primary color, and custom community "Welcome Message."
