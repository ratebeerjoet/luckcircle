# User Experience Flow

## Phase 1: The Invitation
1. Community Manager sends a link: `community.luckcircle.so`
2. User logs in, connects LinkedIn, and answers the "Anti-Networking" prompts.

## Phase 2: The Match
1. AI identifies two other members.
2. User receives a "Luck Alert": *"We found a match. You're meeting Sarah (SaaS Founder) and Jane (Sales Coach) on Tuesday at 10 AM."*

## Phase 3: The Party (Meeting)
1. User enters the web-app video room.
2. **0-5m:** AI Voice/Text introduces everyone: *"Sarah, meet Jane. You both mentioned struggling with hiring your first SDR..."*
3. **5-40m:** Users follow the AI prompts in the sidebar.
4. **45m:** Meeting automatically closes.

## Phase 4: The Ripple Effect
1. User receives a summary: *"You offered to help Jane with her pitch deck. Click here to send the link."*
2. User sees their "Surface Area for Luck" grow on their profile dashboard.
